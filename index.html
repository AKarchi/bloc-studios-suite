🔒 DATA PRIVACY & AUTO-SAVE NOTE

LOCAL-FIRST ISOLATION

How Auto-Save works: Every letter you draft, every task you toggle, and every schedule block you modify triggers an instantaneous mechanical auto-save directly to your browser's local sandbox memory (LocalStorage).

Absolute Isolation: This application is completely local-first. We do not use external cloud servers or database tracking systems. Because your data lives strictly inside your physical machine, no other person on earth can read, view, or open your files.

Device & Browser Boundaries: Data is localized within the browser instance sandbox. It will not sync automatically across devices without hardware export transfers.

⏳ THE EPHEMERAL ARCHITECTURE // SYSTEM TIME DECAY

AUTOMATED DISPOSAL & REFRESH INFRASTRUCTURE

  • The Brain Dump (Transient Buffer): A dedicated, high-utility scratchpad for raw, unfiltered thoughts or clipboard scraps. To force active processing, the buffer operates on a strict 24-hour expiration countdown initialized on entry. You must either migrate your concepts to permanent records or allow them to dissolve completely into the system space.
  • The Shredder (Volatile Disposal): An isolated, low-opacity safety network sitting at the baseline of your core work views. When a Task or permanent Note is deleted via the [DEL] trigger, it is intercepted here instead of vanishing instantly. Items remain restorable for exactly 24 hours from the point of disposal before unrecoverable system decay occurs.
  • The System Pulse (Weekly Vitals): An automated 7-dot visual efficiency tracker that runs purely on calendar state logic. Rather than tracking historical data bloat, it calculates compliance dynamically across the current week (Monday–Sunday) based on your real-time Radar metrics. The entire grid undergoes an absolute automated wipe the millisecond Monday morning initializes.
